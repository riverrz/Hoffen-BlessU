INCENTIVES

Motivation of staff and partners is one of the main components of any business.

GALA DINNER

For most customers, an important part of their event is the Gala Dinner. The Gala Dinner reflects the image, reputation and prestige of your organization or company. Based on budget and wishes, we can advise you from main stream to exclusive and intimate locations, including decorations, entertainment and, of course, catering tailored to your wishes. Imagine inviting your guests or associates to a unique event; a superb dinner in the special atmosphere of an industrial heritage, a modern landmark, an old mansion, a yacht or luxury country estate.

ACTIVITIES

Excursions and activities are often the most memorable moments during a trip. Varying from very unique ancient city tours to rafting on intense rivers in the mountains, entertainment is guaranteed.

HOTELS

You do not need to spend a lot of time looking for the best hotel for you. Our team will select the best hotels, fitting your needs, in Europe for you at competitive prices!

TRANSPORT

Does your group need private transport, for transfer or a longer tour? If so, Incentive Europe offers the most courteous, reliable and comfortable bus service you’ll find. Whether you’re in the city or out in the country, let us make your group’s trip a smooth and memorable experience.

ON-SITE SUPPORT

Incentive Europe offers its clients not only the organization of events at the highest level, but also manages your onsite execution of all details, big and small. Relax and lean back, our professional on site staff takes care the trip goes as planned.
