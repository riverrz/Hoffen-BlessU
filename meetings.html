MEETINGS

Business meetings and negotiations – an integral part of a successful business.

VENUE

The Netherlands offers a wide variety of venues to organize your corporate event; from medieval castles to modern and luxury conference halls. A comfortable and state-of-the-art equipped meeting space or venue is a main driver for successful business meetings and events. Allow your business partners and associates to feel your attention and care, while our experienced team helps you to choose the best venue for your successful meeting.

AV

From simply arranging your presentation material to a high tech recorded product presentation or a simultaneous translated event, rely on us to take care of your audio-visual needs. We will think along with you to find the best provider(s), advising you on the best technical solutions, cost effective options and alternatives, ensuring a smooth and seamless execution.

GALA DINNER

For most customers, an important part of their event is the Gala Dinner. The Gala Dinner reflects the image, reputation and prestige of your organization or company. Based on budget and wishes, we can advise you from main stream to exclusive and intimate locations, including decorations, entertainment and, of course, catering tailored to your wishes. Imagine inviting your guests or associates to a unique event; a superb dinner in the special atmosphere of an industrial heritage, a modern landmark, an old mansion, a yacht or luxury country estate.

HOTEL

You do not need to spend a lot of time looking for the best hotel for you. Our team will select the best hotels, fitting your needs, in Europe for you at competitive prices!

TRANSPORT
